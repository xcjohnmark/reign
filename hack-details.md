# Build-X Hackathon X Cup — Detailed Information

The **X Cup Hackathon** is a 10-day online developer challenge themed around the **World Cup** and built on **X Layer** (OKX’s Layer 2 network). The objective is to build projects that can capture World Cup traffic and convert it into on-chain transactions/users.

* **Organizers**: X Layer (`@XLayerOfficial`)
* **Partners**: Uniswap (`@Uniswap`) & Flap (`@flapdotsh`)
* **Location**: Fully online (remote)

---

## 📅 Timeline
* **Hackathon Period**: May 19, 2026, 23:59 UTC - May 28, 2026, 23:59 UTC
* **Submission Deadline**: May 28, 2026, at 23:59 UTC

---

## 🚀 Tracks
The hackathon encourages projects in the following areas (but is not limited to them):
* Prediction Markets
* Trading
* Social

## ⚙️ Submission & Technical Requirements
To qualify, submissions must meet the following criteria:
- Projects must be World Cup-themed and built on X Layer
- Projects must have a dedicated X (Twitter) account, tag @XLayerOfficial on submission, and post actively throughout the hackathon
- Eligible tracks include (but are not limited to): prediction markets, trading, social, NFT, GameFi, and AI Agent
- At least part of the project must be deployed on X Layer
- Fully remote — no in-person attendance required
- Existing projects are eligible, but must include substantial new development completed during the hackathon period
- Submit the project via 
    
    [Google Form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdj19ZO-gQwLKEz36Z2XDLL7eTdSr-PRXcDmy4p6G2GFvrWKw/viewform?usp=dialog)
    
     before 23:59 UTC on May 28.

## ⚖️ Judging & Rules
The hackathon uses a **dual-track judging model** combining automated **AI scoring** and **human expert evaluation** (scoring independently).

### Scoring Criteria:
* **Innovation**: Degree of differentiation within the World Cup context (mechanisms, gameplay, or approach).
* **Market Potential**: Ability to capture World Cup traffic and drive users and transactions to X Layer.
* **Completion**: Deliverables, demonstrability, and on-chain verifiability of code/contracts.
* **Demo Video**: Direct bonus points (1-3 mins).

### Safety & Legal:
> [!WARNING]
> Wallet addresses and participants will be screened against OFAC, EU, UK, and UN lists before payouts.

* **Intellectual Property**: Participants retain ownership of their IP, but grant X Layer and partners a non-exclusive license to use/promote/report on the submission.
